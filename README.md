# morama

## Overview

**morama** is a simple command-line interface (CLI) application for recording and managing your personal reviews and ratings for movies and TV dramas. Built with Go, morama helps you keep track of what you've watched and how you felt about it â€” all from your terminal.

morama lets you:
- Record reviews and star ratings for movies and dramas.
- Browse and search your personal watch history.
- Categorize and filter entries by title, genre, or rating.
- Easily update or delete entries as your opinions evolve.
